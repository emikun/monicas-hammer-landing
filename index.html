Working together. Empowering you.

DIY projects are fun, empowering, and not to mention economical. Every project is full of possibilities and the potential to customize based on your needs, style, and vision.

But

Most DIY'ers fail to launch their projects and turn their visions into realities. Why? Because a lack of knowledge and/or resources to complete the project aren't clear.

That's where we come in...

What is Monica's Hammer?

Monica's Hammer is a collaborative platform that allows women to work together on home projects, transforming do-it-yourself to do-it-together.

Whether you need help with a project, or want to help someone accomplish their DIY dreams, Monica's Hammer strives to create a safe, fun, and empowering environment where women can work together and collaborate.

How it works

I need help

Have a DIY project you want to accomplish but don't necessarily know where to start?

Step 1

Post the project details for your DIY project online.

Step 2

Accept bids for project suppliers and pick the collaborator you want to work with.

Step 3

Set up a time for you and collaborator to meet. Hammer away!

Step 4

Post your project details and show off your work!

I want to help

Got skills to pay the bills and want to help someone accomplish their DIY dream project?

Step 1

Search for projects based on your unique skill sets.

Step 2

Find a project you like. Place a bid on a project.

Step 3

Set up a time for you and collaborator to meet. Hammer away!

Step 4

Revel in your magnificent work & that you helped!
