DIY projects are fun, empowering, and not to mention economical. Every project is full of possibilities and the potential to customize based on your needs, style, and vision.

But

Most DIY'ers fail to launch their projects and turn their visions into realities. Why? Because a lack of knowledge and/or resources to complete the project aren't clear.

That's where we come in...

What is Monica's Hammer?

Monica's Hammer is a collaborative platform that allows women to work together on home projects, transforming do-it-yourself to do-it-together.

Whether you need help with a project, or want to help someone accomplish their DIY dreams, Monica's Hammer strives to create a safe, fn, and empowering environment where women can work together and collaborate.
